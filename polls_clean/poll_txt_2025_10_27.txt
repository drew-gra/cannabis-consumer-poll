NuggMD Cannabis Consumer Poll
October 16 - October 27, 2025
Sample: 285 respondents | Margin of error: ±5.81%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUESTION: Last week, news broke that Target has started selling THC-infused cannabis beverages in some stores. Does knowing this make you more likely to shop at Target in the future?

RESULTS:
34% - Yes -- but only if my local Target carries the THC-infused products
16% - Yes -- I want to support the retailer more now regardless of which locations sell the products
50% - No, it will not affect my behavior

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

METHODOLOGY: 
Digital questionnaire sent to verified, representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets. Targeted sampling from verified consumer database with multi-point validation including consumption verification, attention checks, and statistical outlier detection.

SAMPLE DETAILS:
- Population: Representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets
- Survey Method: Digital questionnaire via secure online platform
- Confidence Level: 95%
- Z-score: 1.96
