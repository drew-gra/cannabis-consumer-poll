NuggMD Cannabis Consumer Poll
September 12 - September 15 and September 19 - September 21, 2024
Sample: 291 respondents | Margin of error: ±5.74%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUESTION: To what extent can pro-cannabis policy or commitments from the presidential candidates influence your vote this November?

RESULTS:
21% — It can directly influence my vote a little bit
52% — It can directly influence my vote a great deal
10% — Cannabis is the only issue I care about
17% — It cannot influence my vote at all

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

METHODOLOGY: 
Digital questionnaire sent to verified, representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets. Targeted sampling from verified consumer database with multi-point validation including consumption verification, attention checks, and statistical outlier detection.

SAMPLE DETAILS:
- Population: Representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets
- Survey Method: Digital questionnaire via secure online platform
- Confidence Level: 95%
- Z-score: 1.96
