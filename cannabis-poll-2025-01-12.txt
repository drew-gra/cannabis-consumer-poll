NuggMD Cannabis Consumer Poll
January 9-12, 2025
Sample: 381 respondents | Margin of error: ±5.02%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUESTION: Does your cannabis consumption tend to affect your alcohol consumption?

RESULTS:
54% - Yes, I tend to drink less or not at all when I'm consuming cannabis
22% - No, there's no relationship between the two  
14% - It depends on the circumstances
9%  - Yes, I tend to drink more when I'm consuming cannabis

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

METHODOLOGY: 

Digital questionnaire sent to verified, representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets. 

Targeted sampling from verified consumer database with multi-point validation including consumption verification, attention checks, and statistical outlier detection.

SAMPLE DETAILS:

- Population: Representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets
- Survey Method: Digital questionnaire via secure online platform
- Confidence Level: 95%
- Z-score: 1.96
