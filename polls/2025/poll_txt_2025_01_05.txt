NuggMD Cannabis Consumer Poll
January 2 - January 5, 2025
Sample: 806 respondents | Margin of error: ±3.45%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUESTION: What is your biggest frustration as a cannabis consumer?

RESULTS:
31% — Cannabis costs too much
12% — My favorite products are hard to find
22% — I don't always get the desired effects
7% — There aren't convenient dispensary options near me
14% — I feel judged for using cannabis
14% — There aren't any places I'm legally allowed to smoke

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

METHODOLOGY: 
Digital questionnaire sent to verified, representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets. Targeted sampling from verified consumer database with multi-point validation including consumption verification, attention checks, and statistical outlier detection.

SAMPLE DETAILS:
- Population: Representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets
- Survey Method: Digital questionnaire via secure online platform
- Confidence Level: 95%
- Z-score: 1.96
