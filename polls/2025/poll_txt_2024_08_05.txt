NuggMD Cannabis Consumer Poll
August 1 - August 5, 2024
Sample: 460 respondents | Margin of error: ±4.57%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUESTION: Kamala Harris is now the presumptive Democratic presidential nominee after Joe Biden dropped out. How has this impacted your views on November's election?

RESULTS:
40% — I like Harris as the candidate over Biden
3% — I liked Biden as the candidate over other Democratic alternatives
48% — I intend to vote for the Republican candidate
4% — I intend to vote for a third party
6% — I don't have an opinion on the election yet

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

METHODOLOGY: 
Digital questionnaire sent to verified, representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets. Targeted sampling from verified consumer database with multi-point validation including consumption verification, attention checks, and statistical outlier detection.

SAMPLE DETAILS:
- Population: Representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets
- Survey Method: Digital questionnaire via secure online platform
- Respondents: Cannabis consumers who are likely/committed voters
- Confidence Level: 95%
- Z-score: 1.96
