NuggMD Cannabis Consumer Poll
August 8 - September 1, 2024
Sample: 352 respondents | Margin of error: ±5.22%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUESTION: To what extent would you trust cannabis purchased from an unregulated source, such as a delta-8 shop, vape shop, or dealer, to be accurately labeled and free of contaminants?

RESULTS:
6% — I would trust it a great deal
12% — I would have some trust in it
31% — I would have a low level of trust in it
51% — I would not trust it at all

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

METHODOLOGY: 
Digital questionnaire sent to verified, representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets. Targeted sampling from verified consumer database with multi-point validation including consumption verification, attention checks, and statistical outlier detection.

SAMPLE DETAILS:
- Population: Representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets
- Survey Method: Digital questionnaire via secure online platform
- Confidence Level: 95%
- Z-score: 1.96
