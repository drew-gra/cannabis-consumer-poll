NuggMD Cannabis Consumer Poll
October 31 - November 3, 2024
Sample: 474 respondents | Margin of error: ±4.50%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

QUESTION: How does a policy of pre-employment drug testing impact your desire to seek employment with a company?

RESULTS:
74% — I want to work for the company much less
13% — I want to work for the company a little bit less
9% — It does not affect my perception
1% — I want to work for the company a little bit more
3% — I want to work for the company much more

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

METHODOLOGY: 
Digital questionnaire sent to verified, representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets. Targeted sampling from verified consumer database with multi-point validation including consumption verification, attention checks, and statistical outlier detection.

SAMPLE DETAILS:
- Population: Representative sample of cannabis consumers in state-legal markets
- Survey Method: Digital questionnaire via secure online platform
- Confidence Level: 95%
- Z-score: 1.96
